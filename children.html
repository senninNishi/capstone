Benefits for Children

Protect your children, know their benefits!

Print a copy

This list is updated as of 10/23/2021 and may be subject to change. Brgy. Malanday wants to make sure that most of our residents are attended to with their health benefits. For more information please visit our website or contact us 941-6106

Check-Up Examinations Vitamins Deworming Pediatric Dental Immunizations

Description: Make sure your babies are physically fit. Let your doctor check their history to make sure that no future health concerns may affect your children.

Frequency: You may request check-ups from us whenever you feel that your children needs medical attention. We ensure that your little infants are always healthy.

Description: The government recommends all children aged 6 months to 5 years are given vitamin supplements containing vitamins A, C and D every day.

Frequency: Having too much of some vitamins can be harmful. Keep to the dose recommended on the label, and be careful not to give your child 2 supplements at the same time.

For example, do not give them cod liver oil and vitamin drops because cod liver oil also contains vitamins A and D. One supplement on its own is enough, as long as it contains the recommended dose of vitamin D.

Description: Deworming is the process that involves the use of medication to get rid of intestinal parasites like worms. Worm infections, unfortunately, can happen even after best hygiene measures and precautions, particularly, in children. Therefore, it is the responsibility of parents to ensure that their children are free of this dangerous parasitic infection.

Frequency: Deworming should get done twice a year or every six months, starting from the age of two years old.

Description: Let your children show their confident smiles, make sure they always visit their dentist for a routine dental exam.

Frequency: Every 6 months.

The following vaccines are recommended for children and are covered with no out-of-pocket cost.

  • Influenza (Flu)
  • Hepatitis A
  • Hepatitis B

These benefits are identified as free service for all beneficiaries of Brgy. Malanday.

  • Senior Citizens and PWDs are top priority
  • Benefits are available to all beneficiaries of Brgy. Malanday, Marikina City
  • No one shall be charged for these services, if you experienced this please call 941-6106
